Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to build a website?

A: Most small business websites are ready in 1–2 weeks, depending on complexity and content provided.

Q: Do I need to provide my own images and text?

A: You can provide your own, or we can help source professional images and write copy for an additional fee.

Q: Can I update my website myself?

A: Yes — we build with easy-to-edit layouts so you can make minor updates without coding knowledge.

Q: Will my website work on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely. All our websites are fully responsive on phones and tablets.

Q: Can you create a custom design for my business?

A: Yes — each website is tailored to your brand and services to make the best impression on your customers.
